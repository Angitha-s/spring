Ghilli is a 2004 Indian action film about a state-level kabaddi player, Velu, who rescues a woman named Dhanalakshmi from a ruthless local gangster, Muthupandi, who wants to marry her. The story follows Velu's efforts to save her from the villain's relentless pursuit and a week of action-packed chase sequences in Madurai and Chennai. The film is known for its fast-paced action, humor, and iconic hero-villain dynamic.
